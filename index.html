Texus Electronics Executive KPI Analysis

This project was unique as the client company is a leading technology distributor, and a provider of cutting-edge products which includes; laptops, digital cameras, smartphones, headphones, gaming consoles and smartTVs. With customers coming from all over the world, like Europe and Africa, Texus Electronics sought to stay ahead in a competitive market and transition from the traditional Excel reports to a more dynamic and responsive reporting sysytem. We discovered through indepth analysis and visualisation that majority of the customers with the most patronage came from African countries like Kenya, Eygpt, Ethopia etc. The suggestions we gave helped to increase sales in low performing countries by 20% leveraging Microsoft Power BI

Hospital Emergency Room Visit Analysis using Tableau

The client hospital guided by its new medical director wanted to tackle critical challenges within its Emergency Room (ER) and formulate strategic improvements as he has observed a gap in the ER operations that has led to patient dissatisfaction. After analysis using KPIs, we discovered that the patient's wait time for the ER was too long which sometimes led to loss of life. The suggestions given when implememented was able to optimize emergency care and reduce the wait time by 10%.

Analyzing TopBase Customer Churn

TopBase a Telecom client company started experiencing decline in sales which led to recedes in revenue as they were losing customers and would love to understand why this was happening. At the end of the analysis, it was discovered that their customers were dissatisfied with some of their services and also that their competitors had better devices at better prices with a more reliable network. My team and I made recommendations on marketing campaigns and juicey offers that reduced customer churning rates and brought in new customers and increased sales revenue by 10%
